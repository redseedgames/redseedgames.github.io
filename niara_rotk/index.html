FAQs


What is this?

This is a website made for our upcoming visual novel RPG 'Niara:Rebellion Of the King'.


What type of game will it be?

This will be a visual novel RPG. In this game you will have to react to dialogues between characters and also take part in menu based exploration of the game map and turn based battles.


When will it be available?

This game will be released in 2019. Stay in touch with us to get updates on the exact date.


Which platform will it be available in?

This game will be available on Android mobile platform.


Will it be fun?

If you are a fan of visual novels then you will enjoy this game. If you are not a fan of visual novels then you may not enjoy it so much.


Which engine did you make it in?

We are developing the game in Renpy.


How did you program the battles and exploration part?

We used python to program the battles and the exploration part.


Will it be free?

There will be a free and paid version of the game. The free version will let you play till the first chapter. You will have to purchase the paid version in order to play the subsequent chapters of the game.


Where is your company based at?

Our company is based in India.
